ABOUT PROJECT

MemeKiller, an NFT game based on the Binance Smart Chain platform, provides users with an immersive role-play gaming experience in battles with shitcoins. Users may upgrade weapons and add items to increase the warrior’s combat strength and help him win. In addition, gamers may collect NFTs, receive incentives, and communicate with one another.

MemeKiller is a game where everyone may feel like a warrior, battle with shit coins, make money, and have fun while doing so.

NFT Weapons:
More than 100 different legendary weapons are accessible in the form of NFT. Users are urged to seek out the top weapons and destroy their enemies.
Unique items:
Unique amor items are offered as stamina enhancers to increase the strength of your warriors.
Mystery Boxes:
Mystery boxes such as Chinese Melee or Chinese Ward are also included in the game so you can get unique skins, weapons for your warriors. The mystery boxes ranks include normal, rare, super rare and legendary armour/ weapons.
